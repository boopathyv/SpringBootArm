'Aram' as the name says, we, at Aram IAS academy, are relentlessly working towards our vision of producing dutiful and honest bureaucrats to the country. Indian Civil Services being one of the most elite jobs in the country, checks not just a candidate's knowledge but his/her personality as a whole.

At Aram IAS Academy, acknowledging this necessity, we are providing value-based education system to the aspirants to enable them to accomplish their dream. Our humble journey started in the year 2011 and with every passing year, the number of aspirants from Aram IAS academy clearing the prestigious exams has been gliding up. Our success is attributed to the highly experienced and devoted faculties who hail from a wide spectrum of disciplines. Most of the faculties at Aram IAS academy are also proficient authors who have published books of exceptional quality.

We guide aspirants for cracking both UPSC and TNPSC(Group I and Group II) examinations. Aspirants are guided meticulously from Preliminary to Interview (Personality Assessment) level with classes focusing on Prelims(General Studies and CSAT), Mains(General studies and optional subjects) and Interview training.
