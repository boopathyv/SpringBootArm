About the course

‘Practice doesn’t make perfect, perfect practice makes perfect’ - This is true for UPSC exam preparation as well.

Cracking UPSC exam needs a mixture of hard work and smart work. How much information an aspirant retains while appearing for the exam decides his/her probability of success. Retention can be improved only by re-reading as many times as possible as this consolidates memory.

Understanding the difficulties that aspirants face during revision period, ARAM IAS ACADEMY launches this tailor-made course three months prior to the preliminary examination to give a recap to aspirants of what is to be revised to clear the prelims. The course also includes test series which gives the aspirants a complete trust in themselves before appearing for the exam.
